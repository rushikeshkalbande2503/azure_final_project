How We Work

Identification

We identify important components such as people (customers and employees), processes (operations and control), technology.

Specification

Here we Define app requirements, style (custom or pre-packaged). We also Determine whether to buy, develop, or integrate and identify the app.

Development

We Specify the target development environment; integration and synchronisation with the back-end data, quality control and testing.

Security

Here we limit data access, and identify the audience with access to the app and its data.

Installation

Here we basically select the mode of distribution; fulfillment and analytics.

Management

Tracking and monitoring app usage and enforcement of compliance policies.
