Domains & Hosting

Domain name is the address of your website that people type in the browser’s URL bar to visit your website.Web hosting is the place where all the files of your website live. It is like the home of your website where it actually lives.

Description

When someone enters your domain name in a browser, the domain name is translated into the IP address of your web hosting company’s computer. This computer contains your website’s files, and it sends those files back to the users’ browsers.Without domain names, it will not be possible for people to find your website and without web hosting you cannot build a website.
